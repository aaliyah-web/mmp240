World's Best Fired Rice

This Chinese-inspired fried rice recipe is my absolute fave. It’s quick and easy to make, customizable with any of your favorite mix-ins, and so irresistibly delicious.

Ingredients

  • Cooked, chilled rice
  • Eggs
  • Carrots, onions, green onions and peas
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Toasted sesame oil
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Scramble your eggs
  2. Sauté your veggies and garlic
  3. Stir fry rice
  4. Remove pan from heat
  5. Taste and season
  6. Serve!

Other Variations

Here are some classic add-ins: Chicken Fried Rice, Pork Fried Rice, Shrimp Fried Rice, or Kimchi Fried Rice
